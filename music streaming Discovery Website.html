About Hymn

Hymn, the word derived from the word melody

Is an Indian audio streaming and media services provider that was founded on October 19, 2023, by Fareez Raza, Lalhlimtluanga, and Izan Nissar Lone. It has rapidly become one of the largest music streaming service providers in India.

As of November 2023, Hymn boasts a substantial user base, with over 574 million monthly active users. Among these users, there are 226 paying subscribers who enjoy premium features and an ad-free experience.
